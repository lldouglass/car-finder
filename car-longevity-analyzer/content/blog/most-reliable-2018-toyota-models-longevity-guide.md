## Common Issues and Solutions

### Minor Issues Across 2018 Toyota Models

**Infotainment System Software**:
- **Symptoms**: Occasional freezing, slow response times
- **Solution**: Software updates available through dealerships
- **Cost**: Usually covered under warranty or software update campaigns
- **Prevention**: Keep system updated, avoid extreme temperatures

**Paint Quality (Certain Colors)**:
- **Affected**: Some metallic paint colors showing premature fading
- **Solution**: Paint correction services, protective coatings
- **Cost**: $500-2,000 depending on severity and size
- **Prevention**: Regular waxing, garage storage, paint protection film

**Door Handle Chrome Peeling**:
- **Affected**: Models with chrome exterior trim
- **Solution**: Replacement handles or vinyl wrap
- **Cost**: $200-500 per handle
- **Prevention**: Regular cleaning, avoid harsh chemicals

### Model-Specific Considerations

**2018 Camry**:
- **Oil Dilution**: Minor issue in cold climates with short trips
- **Solution**: Extended warm-up periods, highway driving
- **Dashboard Reflection**: Some interiors show dashboard glare
- **Solution**: Dashboard covers, window tinting

**2018 RAV4**:
- **Wind Noise**: Some examples exhibit wind noise at highway speeds
- **Solution**: Weatherstrip adjustment, door seal inspection
- **Rear Differential**: AWD models require 60,000-mile service
- **Solution**: Follow maintenance schedule religiously

**2018 Prius**:
- **12V Battery Life**: Auxiliary battery replacement around 80K miles
- **Solution**: Preventive replacement every 6-8 years
- **Brake Feel**: Regenerative braking affects pedal feel
- **Solution**: Normal operation, driver adaptation period

## Maintenance Schedules for Maximum Longevity

### Standard Maintenance (All 2018 Toyota Models)

**Every 5,000 Miles** (Recommended despite 10K intervals):
- Engine oil and filter change
- Tire rotation and pressure check
- Visual inspection of fluid levels
- Battery terminals and connections check

**Every 15,000 Miles**:
- Engine air filter inspection/replacement
- Cabin air filter replacement
- Brake system inspection
- Exhaust system check

**Every 30,000 Miles**:
- Spark plug inspection (replace if needed)
- Drive belt inspection
- Cooling system inspection
- Suspension component check

**Every 60,000 Miles**:
- Transmission service (fluid change)
- Brake fluid replacement
- Coolant system service
- Differential service (AWD models)

**Physical Inspection Points**:

**Test Drive Checklist**:
- Engine starts quickly and idles smoothly
- Transmission shifts appropriately (CVT should be smooth)
- Brakes feel firm and stop straight
- Steering responsive with no excessive play
