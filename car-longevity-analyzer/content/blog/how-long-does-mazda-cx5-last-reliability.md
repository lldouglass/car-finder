# How Long Does a Mazda CX-5 Last? Complete Reliability Guide

The Mazda CX-5 has established itself as one of the most reliable compact SUVs on the market, combining engaging driving dynamics with solid build quality. With proper maintenance, most CX-5s can be expected to reach **200,000-275,000 miles**, making them excellent long-term ownership choices.

Based on NHTSA reliability data and owner satisfaction surveys, the average Mazda CX-5 lifespan ranges from 14-18 years or 220,000-260,000 miles when properly maintained. The key to CX-5 longevity lies in regular maintenance and understanding generation-specific characteristics.

## Mazda CX-5 Reliability by Generation

### Second Generation (2017-2026): Refined Excellence
- **Expected Lifespan**: 250,000-300,000+ miles
- **Reliability Rating**: Excellent
- **Best Features**: Improved NVH, premium interior, advanced safety

### First Generation (2013-2016): Proven Foundation
- **Expected Lifespan**: 200,000-250,000 miles  
- **Reliability Rating**: Very Good
- **Strengths**: Simple design, proven Skyactiv technology

## Engine and Transmission Analysis

**2.5L Skyactiv-G Engine**: The workhorse powering most CX-5s delivers excellent reliability with conservative engineering and proven durability exceeding 250,000 miles.

**6-Speed Automatic**: Traditional torque converter automatic proves more reliable than CVT competitors, with typical service life of 200,000+ miles.

## Maintenance Requirements

**Critical Service Intervals**:
- Oil changes: Every 7,500 miles (5,000 miles in severe conditions)
- Transmission service: Every 60,000 miles
- All-wheel drive service: Every 60,000 miles
- Spark plugs: Every 75,000 miles

## Common Issues and Solutions

**Minor Issues**: Some interior trim pieces may show wear over time, and the infotainment system occasionally requires updates.

**AWD System**: The i-Activ AWD system is generally reliable but requires proper maintenance of differentials and coupling mechanisms.

## Cost of Ownership

**Annual Maintenance**: $600-900 for routine service
**Major Services**: $800-1,200 every 60,000 miles
**Total 10-Year Cost**: $8,000-12,000 for maintenance and repairs

## Buying Recommendations

**Best Value**: 2017-2019 models offer modern features with proven reliability
**Avoid**: Early 2013 models had minor infotainment issues
**Sweet Spot**: 2018-2020 models for optimal feature/reliability balance

## Conclusion

The Mazda CX-5 represents excellent value in the compact SUV segment, offering reliability that rivals Toyota and Honda while providing superior driving dynamics. With proper maintenance, owners can expect 220,000+ miles of dependable service, making it an excellent choice for long-term ownership.
