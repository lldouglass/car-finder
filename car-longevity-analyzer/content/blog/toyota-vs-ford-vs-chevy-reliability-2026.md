## Quick answer: Which brand is most reliable in 2026?

For mainstream SUVs and trucks in 2026:
1. **Toyota** has the most consistent reliability across model lines.
2. **Ford** has strong truck options and improving trends in some segments.
3. **Chevy** can be competitive on routine cost, but specific V8-era risk areas still matter.

If your decision is purely long-term durability and downtime risk, Toyota usually wins. If your decision is price, towing, and feature mix, Ford or Chevy can still be better values in specific configurations.

- Naturally aspirated Toyota powertrains still reward owners who keep cars 8 to 12 years.
- Resale remains stronger, which lowers total ownership cost even when purchase price is higher.
