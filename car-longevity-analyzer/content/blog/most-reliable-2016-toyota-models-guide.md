## Common Issues and Solutions

### Minor Issues Across 2016 Models

**Dashboard Appearance**:
- **Issue**: Some dashboard surfaces showing minor wear
- **Solution**: Dashboard covers, UV protection
- **Prevention**: Park in shade when possible, window tinting

**Door Handle Chrome**:
- **Affected Models**: Models with chrome exterior trim
- **Issue**: Chrome peeling or pitting over time
- **Solution**: Handle replacement or protective wrapping
- **Cost**: $200-400 per handle depending on model

**Tire Pressure Monitoring System**:
- **Issue**: TPMS sensors requiring battery replacement
- **Timeline**: Typically around 80,000-100,000 miles
- **Solution**: Individual sensor replacement
- **Cost**: $50-75 per sensor plus programming

### Model-Specific Considerations

**2016 Camry Specific**:
- **Dashboard Reflection**: Some interiors show dashboard glare
- **Solution**: Anti-glare dashboard covers available
- **Window Tint**: Helps reduce interior heat and UV damage

**2016 Prius Specific**:

- **Timing**: Normal replacement interval, not a defect
- **Cost**: $150-200 including installation

**2016 RAV4 Specific**:

- **All-Wheel Drive**: Rear differential service every 60,000 miles
- **Ground Clearance**: Excellent for preventing undercarriage damage

## Maintenance Guidelines for Longevity

### Standard Maintenance Schedule

**Every 5,000 Miles**:
- Engine oil and filter change (use manufacturer-specified oil)
- Tire rotation and pressure check
- Visual inspection of fluid levels and leaks
- Battery terminals and connections inspection

**Every 30,000 Miles**:
- Engine air filter replacement
- Cabin air filter replacement
- Brake system inspection and service
- Fuel system cleaning (if needed)

**Every 60,000 Miles**:
- Transmission service (fluid change)
- Spark plug replacement
- Drive belt inspection and replacement
- Cooling system service

### High-Mileage Maintenance (150,000+ Miles)

**Enhanced Service Schedule**:
- Oil changes every 3,000-5,000 miles
- Transmission service every 45,000 miles
- Comprehensive inspection every 15,000 miles
- Cooling system service every 60,000 miles

**Preventive Replacements**:
- Water pump inspection at 150,000 miles
- Timing chain inspection (no belt in these engines)
- Suspension component replacement as needed
- HVAC system maintenance and refresh
